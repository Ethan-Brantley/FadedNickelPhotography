Hey, I'm Ryan

I'm a freelance photographer.

I am an experienced photographer and licensed drone pilot with a keen eye on capturing the breathtaking outdoors. I've been living in Fort Collins for most of my life. I grew up in the Black Hills of South Dakota until I was 7 years old and lived with my family in Colorado ever since. My Grandfather passed his love of photography to my dad who then passed it on to me.

I started shooting photos when I was a kid with those disposable cameras you find in drug stores. I wasn't very good at it but it got me going. When smartphones came on the scene and cameras transitioned to digital, I got my hands on my first iPhone and started shooting pictures of everything since. From then on, I've always had a camera near me wherever I go and I always have my head on a swivel for the next adventure.

I love taking photos. It's a way of capturing the moment and preserving it to enjoy later. I've always been passionate about the outdoors and I love to delve into my creative side.
-Ryan Kelley
