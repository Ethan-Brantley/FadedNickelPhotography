Faded Nickel Photography

Based out of Fort Collins, Colorado, Faded Nickel Photography is your local photography professional. Whether it's capturing the perfect moment to adorn the walls of your home or rendering a captivating view of your property, I have the tools and experience to deliver results.

A camera is a tool and nothing more. Whether you are looking at the macro view of a flower petal or the birds-eye-view of a landscape, the results always depend on the skills and handiwork of the professional behind the camera.

That's where I come in.

Cameras and Typewriters

As the story goes, it was a stormy evening in Havana, over cocktails, when famous author Ernest Hemingway met famous photographer Irving Penn. Impressed by the quality of his work, Hemingway complimented Penn, "Good pictures, what camera do you use?" Penn smiled as he responded, "What typewriter do you use?"

Whether this encounter happened or not, the point is clear: art is not made by camera only. The camera, whether a simple point-and-shoot or a sophisticated precision instrument, is only the tool. It is the craftsman who uses the tool who creates the art.

For each tool I use, I continue to refine my craft to create works of art.
